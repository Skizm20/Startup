Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges.

Is there a free trial?

The Free plan is available forever with no credit card required. Paid plans don't have a trial, but you can cancel anytime.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for yearly plans. Payments are processed securely through Stripe.

Can I get a refund?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 30 days for a full refund.
